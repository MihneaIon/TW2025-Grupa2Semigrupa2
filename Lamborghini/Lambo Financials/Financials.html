2024: RECORD SET IN MAIN KPIS

Lamborghini closed its best-ever year in the main financial metrics, with 10.687 cars delivered and a turnover of €3,09 billion, exceeding for the first time the threshold of 3 billion.
Operating income reached the figure of €835 million, corresponding to a RoS of 27%, consolidating the brand’s position among the most profitable global players in the luxury goods sector.

Data are referred to Lamborghini Group (Automobili Lamborghini S.p.A. and Automobili Lamborghini America LLC) consolidated KPIs.
